Job Description

    We need an expert WordPress and PHP developer on Monthly basis.

    Candidate should be expert in the following areas:

    1- WordPress template customization.

    2- WordPress plugin development in customization.

    3- PHP Development and Customization.

    4- HTML Conversion and CSS customization etc.

    5- WooCommerce knowledge and its complete understanding that how to make a template compatible with WooCommerce, how to effectively used WooCommerce Add On Plugins to make enhanced functionality.

    Candidate should must be one who thinks out of the box. Should be the one who has deep insight and can create websites based on the provided templates.

    Most of the time, developer will be given a topic about the website, and he will have to find WordPress templates on themeforest and will have to recommend us and then we will select and provide the template and developer will have to start working on the template to make it a working beautiful website by choosing slides and images etc (if not provided) from google or image providing websites.

    Things we require from developer:

    1- He will have to log his time in oDesk work diary so we may monitor that he is really working on our projects.

    2- He needs to be honest and loyal during all his work timings (Monday - Friday 9am - 6pm with one hour break)

    3- This job is based on Monthly salary. Its not a one time project. Its just steady monthly salary.

    4- Developer will have to work on all the things he will be asked to do and needs to be creative and self motivated, who may work with minimal supervision.

    5- Should be expert to make the layouts perfect in terms of responsiveness and coloring.

    Developers from Pakistan are highly Welcome.

    Please include "I agree its 300$/ month Salary Job" at the top of application so i may know you have read all the terms.

    Thanks.
